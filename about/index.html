About this blog

This is my techie notes about various things I come across and have time to write about - notes to self that might be useful to someone else.

Currently, the blog is generated by Hugo and hosted at Github Pages. The theme is a little modified version of digitalcraftsman’s hugo-minimalist-theme, which in turn is based on Raphael Riegger’s MinimalisticBlogTheme.

Lightly Seared On The Reality Grill is a General Contact Unit type sentient space ship in Ian M. Banks’ (RIP) Culture universe.

About me

I am a system administrator / DevOps / SRE person, who has been around the block a couple of times. I have been working on internet facing production environments all my career, ever since phone modems were a thing. My LinkedIn profile has the sordid details. I have also worked as DJ, luggage porter, antiquarian bookseller, tourist guide, paint factory worker, office worker, office manager, teacher, and janitor. Come to think of it, all of those things prepared me for this career.

Linux is my home, as well as bread and butter. I’m about to hit 20 years of Linux desktop use (we are the few, the proud, and the frustrated (at times)). Fedora has been my daily driver for several years apart from a jaunt with Ubuntu. My personal servers tend to be CentOS, the little ARM boards Debian or Ubuntu.

I like penguins, pythons, clouds, puppets, FTL phones, and cats. #1 in my bucket list is going into orbit - preferably temporarily.
